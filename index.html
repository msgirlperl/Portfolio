Hi there!

I'm Miriam Speert Crowley.

I began my undergraduate degree at Colgate University as an art major but found myself signing up for math class after math class; it didn't hurt that the enrollment lines at the registrar's office weren't long either ;-)

After applying for a summer internship after my sophomore year, I was encouraged to take a computer science course. When I returned to school in the fall, I signed up for COSI 101 and was floored. What fun this was! I high-tailed it to the CS department chair's office and asked him how I could work this into my math major. He helped me create my own combined major of Math/Computer Science.

I've always loved puzzles; my dad and I used to enjoy working on the weekly Car Talk puzzlers together, and as a kid he would ask me if I wanted to 'play algebra'. Looking back, it only seems natural that he was helping me along on my career path to get to work on and solve puzzles every day.


... more to come.. :-)
